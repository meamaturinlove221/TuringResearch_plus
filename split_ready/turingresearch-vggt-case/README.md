## What This Is

This is a case-study repository bundle showing how TuringResearch was dogfooded
on a VGGT human-prior research workflow.

It demonstrates:

- evidence management;
- route changes;
- failure and blocker tracking;
- advisor pack structure;
- redaction and claim safety;
- what remains human research work.
