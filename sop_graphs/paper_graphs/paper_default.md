# TuringResearch Plus SOP: Paper Graph

Graph type: `paper`

Inputs:

- `ResearchBrief`
- `LiteratureSurveyArtifact`
- `ExperimentReport`

Outputs:

- `ArticleBlock`
- `PaperDraft`

Quality gates:

- Every block has EvidenceRef.
- Required figures are available.

Failure gates:

- ExperimentReport missing.
- Missing evidence refs.
