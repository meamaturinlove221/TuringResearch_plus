# TuringResearch Plus v0.2.0 Sprint 2 Risk Register

Status: scope locked.

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hard gates duplicate existing checks instead of simplifying them. | Medium | Medium | Extract only repeated Sprint 1 gate patterns and keep old tests as regression coverage. | `turingresearch-master-orchestrator` |
| Route DSL becomes too broad. | High | Medium | Keep v0.2 scope VGGT-focused and dry-run only. Defer live execution and generic cloud routing. | `turingresearch-fusion-experiment-execution` |
| Failure taxonomy becomes subjective. | Medium | Medium | Require canonical labels, severity mapping, examples, and deterministic normalization tests. | `turingresearch-fusion-stress-test` |
| Method cards overstate paper claims. | High | Medium | Require provenance, EvidenceRef, and `requires-real-paper` fallback when input paper evidence is incomplete. | `turingresearch-paper-writing-pipeline` |
| Figure-to-Architecture fabricates architecture edges. | High | Medium | Label outputs as draft, preserve figure provenance, and emit unsupported-edge warnings. | `turingresearch-paper-figure-asset-pipeline` |
| Sprint 2 accidentally reopens live adapter work. | Medium | Low | Keep live Semantic Scholar and real citation graph expansion deferred in final scope and tests. | `turingresearch-master-orchestrator` |
| Private VGGT paths leak into fixtures. | High | Low | Keep `local_project_links.yaml` private and default tests fixture-only. | `turingresearch-qa-release` |
| Old missing VGGT evidence gets promoted. | High | Medium | Regression tests must keep V260 hard-blocked, V999 non-final, and SparseConv3D success unclaimed. | `turingresearch-master-orchestrator` |
| Paper-to-Method Card depends on incomplete PDF extraction. | Medium | Medium | Allow `requires-human-review` / `requires-real-paper`; do not block route/gate work on PDF completeness. | `turingresearch-pdf-markdown-core` |
| Scope creep into OCR, UI, sync, or Modal live execution. | High | Medium | Enforce non-goals in docs, contracts, and review checklist. | `turingresearch-master-orchestrator` |

## Release Blockers

- No contract exists for a selected Sprint 2 feature.
- A selected feature cannot serialize to both JSON and Markdown.
- A route or method-card output writes planned work as observed.
- SparseConv3D success is claimed without evidence.
- A default test requires network, API keys, private VGGT paths, or real VGGT
  execution.
- A sync adapter or live execution path is added under Sprint 2.

## Human Review Triggers

- Ambiguous paper provenance.
- Missing local visual evidence.
- Missing route evidence.
- Any advisor-facing phrasing that sounds like proof rather than planned work.
- Any architecture draft with inferred edges not backed by a Method Card or
  figure provenance.
