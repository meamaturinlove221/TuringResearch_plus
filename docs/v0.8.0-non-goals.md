# v0.8.0 Non-goals

Status: planning draft.

Round: 149.

v0.8 planning does not include:

- SaaS.
- Cloud-hosted user accounts.
- Default external network exposure for dashboards.
- Automatic upload of private research data.
- Automatic remote experiment execution.
- Unknown third-party plugin execution.
- Automatic community plugin installation.
- A claim that policy-level sandboxing is equivalent to OS-level isolation.
- Legal compliance guarantees.
- Final paper writing without human approval.
- Automatic final abstract, result, or conclusion generation.
- Promotion of fake/demo/planned material to observed evidence.
- Publishing private datasets, model files, advisor feedback, or raw artifacts.
- License bypass.
- Marketplace publishing without a later release gate.
- Any claim that SparseConv3D succeeded without real evidence.
- Reintroduction of prior project naming.

## Explicit Boundaries

- Local server dashboard means local-first project browsing, not a hosted
  product.
- Paper writing beta means evidence-linked draft assistance, not autonomous
  authorship.
- Public plugin registry draft means metadata and review workflow, not safe
  runtime execution.
- OS-level sandbox research means threat modeling and test design until a
  later scope lock approves implementation.
- Case studies must be demo-safe or evidence-reviewed before publication.
- Compliance expansion remains a review aid and cannot replace legal review.
