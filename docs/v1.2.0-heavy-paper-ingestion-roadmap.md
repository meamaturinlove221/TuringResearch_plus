# v1.2.0 Heavy Paper Ingestion Roadmap

Status: future roadmap.

Round: 175 upstream refresh.

The Neocortica-Scholar signal around arXiv PDF plus MinerU fallback is useful
but too heavy for v1.0. This roadmap keeps heavy paper ingestion out of v1.0 and
parks it as a v1.2 research item.

## Why Not v1.0

- Heavy PDF ingestion can introduce large dependencies.
- PDF/OCR/miner pipelines can be slow and flaky.
- Live source fallback can blur fake/live boundaries.
- Extracted equations, figures, and tables still require human validation.
- License and redistribution risk can increase with source downloads.

## v1.2 Exploration Areas

- optional heavy PDF backend;
- arXiv PDF fallback only with explicit opt-in;
- local cache policy;
- extraction provenance and page map;
- figure/table reuse safety;
- human-review gates before paper writing.

## Required Gates Before Implementation

- dependency review;
- privacy/security audit;
- license review;
- fake fixture path;
- skipped-by-default tests when backend is missing;
- no final paper conclusion generation.

## Decision

MinerU / heavy paper ingestion remains a v1.2 candidate and must not be folded
into v1.0 public launch scope.
