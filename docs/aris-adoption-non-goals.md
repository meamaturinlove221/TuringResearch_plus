# ARIS Adoption Non-goals

Status: non-goal register.

Round: 233.

ARIS adoption is not part of the v1.2 implementation line.

## v1.2 Non-goals

- no ARIS cross-model review loop;
- no ARIS meta-optimize;
- no ARIS proof-checker;
- no ARIS paper-writing automation;
- no ARIS paper resubmit pipeline;
- no ARIS session stop hook;
- no autonomous claim audit runtime;
- no automatic result-to-claim verification;
- no model-only experiment audit;
- no replacement for human review;
- no new remote execution path;
- no default live networking;
- no automatic evidence ledger mutation;
- no automatic final paper generation.

## Adoption Preconditions For v1.4+

Before any ARIS-inspired item can move from study to adoption, it needs:

- design spec;
- threat model;
- safety review;
- fake/default tests;
- privacy/security gate;
- regression gate;
- human-review boundary;
- no evidence mutation without review;
- no final claim generation.

## Rejected Shortcut

Do not treat ARIS as a shortcut for:

- paper writing;
- experiment validation;
- proof of correctness;
- advisor replacement;
- release approval;
- automatic research completion.
