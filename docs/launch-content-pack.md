## Positioning

TuringResearch Plus is a local-first Research OS for evidence-led research
workflows. It keeps evidence, artifacts, experiment routes, paper review,
dashboards, advisor packs, plugin safety, and privacy gates connected.

## One-line Description

Local-first Research OS for evidence ledgers, artifact audits, paper review,
advisor packs, dashboards, plugin safety, and privacy-first research workflows.

## 30-second Launch Pitch

Research projects get scattered fast: papers, notes, artifacts, routes,
dashboards, advisor feedback, and claims drift apart. TuringResearch Plus turns
that into a local, reviewable workflow. It separates planned work from observed
evidence, audits artifacts before they support claims, scaffolds paper review
without inventing conclusions, and packages project state into dashboards and
advisor packs. It is fake/demo-first by default, live adapters are optional,
and human review stays central.

## What To Emphasize

- local-first workflow;
- evidence and artifact discipline;
- paper/research review without final-result fabrication;
- route DSL and hard gates;
- dashboard and advisor review surfaces;
- plugin manifest and safety gates;
- public demo and VGGT public-safe dogfooding case;
- tests, contracts, privacy gates, and release discipline.

## What Not To Say

- Do not say it automates research.
- Do not say it writes final papers.
- Do not say it replaces advisors.
- Do not say VGGT or SparseConv3D succeeded.
- Do not invent users.
- Do not invent stars.
- Do not invent benchmarks.
- Do not imply split repositories already exist.
