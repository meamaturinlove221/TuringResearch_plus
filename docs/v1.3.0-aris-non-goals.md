# v1.3.0 ARIS Non-goals

Status: study non-goals.

Round: 257.

v1.3 is an ARIS study track, not an ARIS merge.

## Non-goals

- No full ARIS runtime merge.
- No autonomous research loop.
- No cross-model review production runtime.
- No proof correctness guarantee.
- No automatic paper writing.
- No automatic paper resubmission.
- No automatic experiment execution.
- No remote execution orchestration.
- No default live networking.
- No evidence ledger mutation without review.
- No replacement for master orchestrator review.
- No replacement for human advisor review.
- No public claim that ARIS features are ready for production use.

## Allowed Study Outputs

- design notes;
- evaluation matrix;
- risk register;
- fake fixtures;
- deterministic local prototypes;
- threat model;
- go/no-go report.

## Boundary

Any future adoption must happen after a later scope lock and safety gate. v1.3
does not grant permission to implement production ARIS behavior.
