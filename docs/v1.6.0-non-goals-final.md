# v1.6.0 Non-goals Final

Status: final scope lock.

Round: 361.

v1.6 is a Public Release Execution Pack. It intentionally does not expand into
automation-heavy or research-claim-heavy surfaces.

## Final Non-goals

- Do not implement ARIS.
- Do not make networking default.
- Do not automatically create GitHub child repositories.
- Do not automatically publish PyPI.
- Do not automatically create tags.
- Do not automatically publish a GitHub release.
- Do not automatically deploy public docs.
- Do not automatically execute remote commands.
- Do not upload private data.
- Do not package raw data.
- Do not execute unknown plugins.
- Do not guarantee public attention, stars, or adoption.

## Required Language

Use:

- `ready for manual review`;
- `workflow draft`;
- `execution pack`;
- `smoke ready`;
- `build-ready`;
- `not published`;
- `not deployed`;
- `not created`;
- `ARIS deferred`.

Avoid:

- `deployed` unless a human has verified deployment;
- `published` unless a human has verified publication;
- `created` for child repositories unless a human has created and verified
  them;
- `observed` for planned, fake, demo, or review-only material.
