# v1.1.0 Paper Writing Beta Scope

Status: scope locked.

Round: 221.

The v1.1 paper writing beta upgrades the v1.0 paper scaffold into a safer,
more connected review workflow. It remains a beta assistant for section
planning and evidence-linked draft material. It does not write a final paper.

## Scope Decision

Decision: `PAPER WRITING BETA SCOPE LOCKED`.

The beta may assemble reviewable paper sections from existing local project
materials, but every generated surface must preserve evidence status, missing
result warnings, citation review status, unsafe claim blocking, and human
review requirements.

## Included Capabilities

### 1. Section Assembly

Assemble review-only paper sections from existing scaffold outputs:

- outline;
- method skeleton;
- related-work skeleton;
- experiment skeleton;
- limitation notes;
- advisor pack decisions.

The output is a structured beta draft surface, not camera-ready prose.

### 2. Evidence-linked Paragraphs

Generate paragraph candidates only when each paragraph records:

- evidence references;
- source section;
- claim status;
- missing evidence;
- human review notes.

Paragraphs without evidence remain marked as draft or blocked.

### 3. Unsafe Claim Guard

Carry forward unsafe claim lists from:

- paper scaffold;
- related-work draft assistant;
- experiment section builder;
- case-study claim guard.

The beta must block unsupported claims, including unverified experiment
success and SparseConv3D success.

### 4. Missing Result Table Guard

Result tables remain blocked unless required result evidence exists. The beta
may export a missing-table checklist, but must not invent numbers, metrics,
visual boards, or ablation results.

### 5. Citation Status Guard

Citation candidates must keep their review state visible:

- fake/manual note;
- requires real paper review;
- reviewed;
- blocked.

The beta must not promote fixture notes into citation-grade references.

### 6. Advisor-to-paper Bridge

Advisor packs can be mapped into paper actions:

- what section needs review;
- which claim needs evidence;
- which result table is missing;
- which limitation should be preserved.

Advisor content remains review input, not final paper text.

### 7. Paper Outline Export

Export a beta outline with section status, evidence refs, missing items,
blocked claims, and next human actions.

- `advisor_to_paper_bridge.md`

## Safety Requirements

- Every beta output must include `requires_human_review`.
- Planned work must stay planned.
- Fake/demo evidence must not become observed evidence.
- Missing results must remain visible.
- Unsafe claims must remain blocked.
- Citation-grade status must be explicit.
- Live paper download is out of scope.

## Relationship To Existing v1.0 Pieces

- Extends `docs/paper-writing-scaffold.md`.
- Uses `docs/paper-assembly-gate.md` as the release gate.
- Preserves `docs/method-section-builder.md` boundaries.
- Preserves `docs/related-work-draft-assistant.md` citation safety.
- Preserves `docs/experiment-section-builder.md` missing result guards.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Beta outputs are reviewable and evidence-linked.
- Missing evidence is never hidden.
- Result tables remain blocked when evidence is missing.
- Unsupported claims are blocked.
- Citation status remains visible.
- No final paper, final abstract, or final conclusion is generated.
