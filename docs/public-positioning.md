# Public Positioning

Status: positioning draft.

Round: 157.

TuringResearch Plus should be presented as a local-first Research OS for
evidence-led research operations.

## One-Line Positioning

TuringResearch Plus is a local-first Research OS that helps researchers manage
evidence, artifacts, routes, paper review, dashboards, advisor packs, plugins,
privacy gates, and replay checks without pretending to automate research
judgment.

## Public Narrative

The project exists because research workflows often scatter across notebooks,
messages, cloud folders, papers, run logs, dashboards, and half-remembered
claims. TuringResearch pulls those surfaces into a review-first local system:

- evidence status is explicit;
- artifacts are audited;
- planned routes are separated from observed results;
- paper scaffolds keep missing evidence visible;
- advisor packs summarize what needs a decision;
- plugin and live surfaces are gated by safety policy;
- public demos stay fake/demo and privacy-safe.

## What To Lead With

1. Local-first Research OS.
2. Evidence ledger and artifact audit.
3. Route DSL and failure taxonomy.
4. Paper intelligence with human review.
5. Advisor pack and static dashboard.
6. Plugin manifest and sandbox policy.
7. VGGT dogfooding case study.
8. Privacy-first public demo.

## What Not To Claim

- autonomous research completion;
- automatic experiment execution;
- final paper writing;
- legal compliance approval;
- VGGT experiment success;
- SparseConv3D success;
- replacement for advisor or human review;
- default live networking.

## Audience

- researchers managing multi-stage projects;
- small labs that need transparent project status;
- advisors reviewing evidence, artifacts, and next actions;
- tool builders exploring local-first research workflows;
- internship or portfolio reviewers evaluating engineering breadth.

## Public Proof Points

- fake/demo public suite;
- VGGT dogfooding case with redaction and claim guard;
- v0.7 full fake replay;
- privacy, compliance, and quality gates;
- plugin loading and compatibility checks that do not execute unknown code;
- dashboard/export quality gates.

## Tone

The project should sound ambitious but honest. It is a serious research
workflow system, not a magic scientist. The most credible message is that it
makes uncertainty and missing evidence visible.
