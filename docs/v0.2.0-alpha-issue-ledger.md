## Issue Categories

- `bug`: behavior or repository state that could break users or review.
- `missing test`: known coverage gap.
- `docs gap`: documentation that is missing or stale.
- `contract drift`: mismatch risk between proposed contracts and public API.
- `naming risk`: risk of legacy project naming returning.
- `fake/live boundary risk`: risk that fake or planned artifacts are presented
  as observed results.
- `VGGT evidence risk`: risk that VGGT claims lack real artifacts.
- `packaging risk`: install, import, version, or metadata risk.
- `upstream watch risk`: risk from missing or stale upstream comparison.
