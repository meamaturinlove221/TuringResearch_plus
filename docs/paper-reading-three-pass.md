# Paper Reading Three-Pass

Status: v0.3 Sprint 2 minimal template.

The three-pass reading template follows a conservative Keshav-style workflow.
It is a planning and extraction scaffold, not proof of full paper review.

## Pass 1: Bird's-Eye Scan

- Read title, abstract, introduction, section headings, figures, and conclusion.
- Record task, claimed contribution, and relevance to VGGT.
- Decide whether the paper deserves deeper review.

## Pass 2: Content Grasp

- Read method and experiment sections closely enough to extract inputs,
  outputs, representation, training objective, and inference pipeline.
- Draft a `PaperMethodCard`.
- Record borrow / not-copy notes and collision-risk questions.

## Pass 3: Deep Understanding

- Reconstruct method mechanics, assumptions, and failure modes.
- Map SMPL / SMPL-X, voxel, tri-plane, token, and geometry relevance to VGGT.
- Update collision notes only after checking evidence.

## Outputs

- Method card
- Collision notes
- Borrow / not-copy list
- VGGT mapping

All outputs require human review before they become related-work claims.
