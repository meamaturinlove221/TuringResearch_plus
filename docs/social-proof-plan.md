# Social Proof Plan

Status: planning draft.

Round: 163.

This plan describes honest social proof for a future public launch. It does not
invent users, stars, benchmarks, offers, endorsements, or affiliations.

## Allowed Social Proof

- visible test summaries;
- release gate reports;
- public demo outputs;
- VGGT dogfooding case-study draft;
- architecture diagram;
- portfolio one-pager;
- clearly labeled roadmap;
- maintainer-written lessons learned;
- real future user feedback only after permission.

## Not Allowed

- fake user quotes;
- fake lab adoption;
- fake benchmark wins;
- fake star targets presented as expected outcomes;
- fake internship or offer association;
- fake production usage;
- fake research success claims.

## Launch Copy Pattern

Use:

> Built as a local-first Research OS for evidence-led workflows, with public
> demo fixtures, safety gates, and a VGGT dogfooding case.

Avoid:

> Used by many researchers.

Avoid unless true and documented:

> Adopted by labs.

## Future Feedback Process

If users provide feedback:

1. ask permission before quoting;
2. record date and context;
3. avoid editing feedback into stronger claims;
4. keep quotes separate from benchmark or research claims;
5. remove quotes on request.
