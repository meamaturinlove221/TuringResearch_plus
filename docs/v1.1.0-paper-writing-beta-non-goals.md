# v1.1.0 Paper Writing Beta Non-goals

Status: locked.

Round: 221.

The paper writing beta is intentionally narrow. It should improve review
surfaces without pretending to replace research judgment or paper authorship.

## Non-goals

- Do not write the final paper.
- Do not generate camera-ready text.
- Do not generate a final abstract.
- Do not generate a final conclusion.
- Do not fabricate experiment results.
- Do not fabricate metrics, tables, ablations, or figures.
- Do not promote fake/demo evidence to observed evidence.
- Do not claim SparseConv3D success without real evidence.
- Do not bypass human review.
- Do not automatically download papers.
- Do not enable live adapters by default.
- Do not access private local paths.
- Do not require VGGT private data.
- Do not produce legal or compliance advice.

## Deferred To Later Versions

- Citation retrieval and live paper source fallback.
- Heavy PDF extraction or MinerU-style fallback.
- Camera-ready prose polish.
- Full LaTeX paper export.
- Reviewer-response automation.
- Multi-author collaboration workflow.

## Required Wording

All paper beta docs and outputs should say that they are review surfaces. They
must not say that TuringResearch automatically writes a finished paper.
