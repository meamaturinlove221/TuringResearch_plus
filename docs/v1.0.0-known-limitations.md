# v1.0.0 Known Limitations

Status: release-candidate limitations.

Round: 195.

TuringResearch Plus v1.0.0 is a local-first research workflow system, not a
hosted research platform.

## Limitations

- No SaaS.
- No cloud user system.
- No automatic experiment execution.
- No automatic final paper writing.
- Live adapters are optional and disabled by default.
- Plugin execution is restricted; unknown third-party plugins are not executed.
- Plugin sandboxing is policy-level, not OS-level sandboxing.
- Compliance assistant output is not legal advice.
- Paper deep review requires human validation.
- Optional PDF/PPTX backends may skip gracefully when dependencies are missing.
- Split repositories are not physically created by default.
- Campaign routing is deterministic and local; it does not replace the master
  orchestrator.
- Pod context lifecycle is safety planning only; it does not run SSH, Modal,
  tmux, or remote commands.
- No guarantee of GitHub star growth.

## Claim Boundaries

- Do not claim automatic research completion.
- Do not claim final paper conclusions without human review.
- Do not claim VGGT or SparseConv3D experiment success without evidence.
- Do not mark planned or fake/demo output as observed evidence.
