# Three-Pass Reading Demo

Status: fake/demo only.

This demo applies a Keshav-style three-pass reading workflow to a cached paper
note. It produces a reading plan, Five Cs report, method mapping, limitations,
and human-review checklist.

Files:

- `cached_note.md`: local fake paper note.
- `reading_plan.md`: generated-style three-pass plan snapshot.
- `five_cs_report.md`: Category, Context, Correctness, Contributions, Clarity.
- `method_mapping.md`: review-only mapping from notes to method-card fields.
- `limitations.md`: safety and review limitations.

The demo does not claim complete paper reading, final conclusions, verified
citations, or camera-ready paper text.
