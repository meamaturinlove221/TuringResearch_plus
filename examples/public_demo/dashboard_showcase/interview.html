TuringResearch is organized as a local-first Research OS: docs, examples, contracts, tests, dashboards, and review-only runtime surfaces all reinforce the same safety model.

Safety Gates

The repo treats safety as product infrastructure: no default network, no unsafe remote execution, no private data, and no planned result promoted to observed evidence.

The main repo remains the flagship, while case/demo child repos are prepared as manual packs instead of being auto-created.

Why ARIS Deferred

ARIS stays out of the default implementation because cross-model review, proof checking, meta optimization, and paper claim audit would add research-automation and claim-authority risk.

  • docs/aris-still-deferred-v1.4.md
