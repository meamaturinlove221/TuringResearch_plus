## Summary

This academic workflow output models research as a routed campaign rather than a single prompt. A research system should know what kind of work is being requested, what preconditions are required, what skills or tools apply, and what artifacts should be produced.

## Campaign Shape

A campaign entry should include:

- campaign name;
- research intent;
- prerequisites;
- required inputs;
- recommended skills;
- output artifacts;
- quality gates;
- failure modes;
- next-route options.
