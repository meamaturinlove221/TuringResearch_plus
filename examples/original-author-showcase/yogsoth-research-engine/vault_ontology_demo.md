## Summary

This academic workflow output treats research memory as a structured knowledge system. Instead of leaving paper notes, hypotheses, failures, and decisions as scattered Markdown files, the workflow organizes them as linked concepts, claims, evidence, aliases, and edges.

## Concepts

A research vault should track:

- concept pages;
- claim pages;
- evidence references;
- method cards;
- experiment plans;
- failure reports;
- decision logs;
- aliases and related terms;
- weak or missing graph edges.

## Ontology Checks

Useful checks include:

- dangling links;
- orphan pages;
- duplicate aliases;
- missing edges;
- unsupported claims;
- stale evidence;
- concepts without definitions;
- methods without experiment routes.
