# TulingResearch Plus Figure Index

This index tracks paper-ready figures, tables, captions, and diagram assets.

## Asset Roots

- `paper/figures/`
- `paper/captions/`
- `paper/tables/`

## Registry

- `paper/paper_asset_registry.yaml`

## Readiness Rules

- No orphan figure.
- Every paper figure must have a caption.
- Every figure must link to at least one ArticleBlock.
- PDF extracted figures must record the original PDF source.
